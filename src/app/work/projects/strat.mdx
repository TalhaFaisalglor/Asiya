- **Comprehensive Content Strategy:** Developed and implemented end-to-end content strategies, including content creation, scheduling, distribution, and performance analysis.
- **Outsourcing Management:** Efficiently managed teams of freelancers and agencies for content writing, graphic design, video editing, software development, data entry, medical billing, digital marketing, customer support, and e-commerce support.
- **Business Operations:** Optimized processes for project management, client communications, financial management, and operational workflows.
- **Scalable Solutions:** Delivered scalable content and operations solutions tailored to the needs of startups, SMEs, and large enterprises.
